FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is it free?

Yes, the extension is free to install and includes 3 exports per day. For unlimited exports, Pro plans start at $2.99/month.

What's included in the free plan?

Up to 3 exports per day, full .docx formatting, math formulas, images, and all three export modes (full chat, Gemini replies only, or selective). Deep Research export is a Pro feature.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, cancel anytime from your account. Your Pro access stays active until the end of the billing period, then reverts to the free plan. We also offer a 7-day money-back guarantee — no questions asked.

Does it work with math and formulas?

Yes. LaTeX and MathML formulas are converted into proper Word equations that stay editable.

Are images from Gemini included?

Yes. Both AI-generated images and images you uploaded are embedded directly into the .docx file.

Do you collect my chat data?

No. Everything runs locally in your browser. Your conversations never leave your device. We only store your email and subscription status to manage your Pro access.

Which browsers are supported?

Any Chromium-based browser: Chrome, Edge, Brave, Opera, and Arc.

Where can I send feedback or feature requests?

Email us at Fatiext2000@gmail.com — we read every message.
