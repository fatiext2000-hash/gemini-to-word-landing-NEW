Privacy Policy

Fatima's GeminiTools · Last updated: June 18, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Fatima's GeminiTools ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the "Export Gemini Chats to Word" Chrome extension ("the Extension").

By using the Extension, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

Google Account Information. When you sign in with Google to activate a Pro subscription, we collect your Google account email address and display name. This is used solely to identify your account and manage your subscription status.

Payment Information. Payments are processed by Dodopayments. We do not store your credit card or payment details. Dodopayments may collect billing information in accordance with their own Privacy Policy.

Usage Data. We use Google Analytics to collect anonymized usage data such as install events and export counts. This data cannot be used to identify individual users.

Chat Content. The Extension processes your Gemini chat content locally in your browser to generate Word documents. We do NOT transmit, store, or access the content of your chats on our servers.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Verify your Pro subscription status
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve the Extension based on anonymized usage patterns
  • Communicate important updates

4. Data Storage

Your email address and subscription status are stored securely on our server hosted on Railway (railway.app). We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except as necessary to provide the service.

Your local extension data (daily usage count, Pro status) is stored in Chrome's local storage on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Google OAuth 2.0 — for sign-in authentication
  • Dodopayments — for payment processing
  • Google Analytics — for anonymized usage statistics
  • Railway — for server hosting

6. Data Retention

We retain your email address and subscription status for as long as you have an active account. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

7. Children's Privacy

The Extension is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this document.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at Fatiext2000@gmail.com.
